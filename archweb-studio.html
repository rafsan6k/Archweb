FAQ

Common
Questions

How long does it take to build my website? +
Most projects are completed within 2–3 weeks from kickoff. Starter typically takes 10 days, Growth 2–3 weeks, Premium 3–5 weeks depending on complexity.
Do I own the website after it's built? +
100% yes. Once final payment is made, you own everything — the design, the code, the domain, the hosting. No lock-ins, no platform fees, no strings attached.
What if I need changes after launch? +
Every package includes revision rounds. After launch we offer affordable maintenance packages or hourly rates for ongoing updates.
Do you work with my industry? +
We've built for restaurants, clinics, law firms, gyms, e-commerce brands, SaaS startups, coaches, and consultants. If you have a business, we can build it.
What do you need from me to get started? +
Your brand assets (logo, colors), content (text, images), and goals. A 15-minute discovery call is all it takes to kick things off.
Is the website mobile-friendly and fast? +
Absolutely. Every site is fully responsive and performance-optimized. We target Google PageSpeed above 90 on mobile and 95+ on desktop.
